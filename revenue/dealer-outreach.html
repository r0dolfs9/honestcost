Dealer outreach.

Twelve targets, named where possible, with three templates each. Send Tier 1.5 first, BMW / Möller last. You're not pitching a tool — you're pitching a calmer customer in their showroom.

Honesty note on contact data. I don't have verified inboxes for marketing leads inside each dealer group. The table below names the company + the role you're targeting; finding the human is the first 10 minutes of your week. LinkedIn is the fastest path — search "[company] marketing" filtered to Latvia. The role names are the right ones; the personal names are yours to confirm.

The three-touch sequence

  • Day 0 — LinkedIn DM · Short, personalised. One sentence about their recent ad / model launch.
  • Day +2 — Email (same day if no LinkedIn reply) · The pitch deck attached, screenshot of their car winning on HonestCost, one specific number.
  • Day +7 — Single follow-up · Two sentences. Reply-or-die. Never a third touch.
Touch 01 · LinkedIn DM
~70 words · LV preferred
Personal, specific, no link.
Goal: a reply. Not a meeting yet. Not a click. Just a reply.
Sveiks, [Vārds]! Redzēju, ka [Greenmotors] aktīvi reklamē [Octavia 1.5 TSI] šomēnes. Aizpagāj nedēļā taisīju nelielu rīku, kas Latvijas jauno auto pircējiem rāda 5 gadu patieso cenu — ne tikai mēneša maksājumu. Iemēģināju [jūsu Octavia] pret [VW Golf] — sanāca, ka jūsējā uzvar par €1 200 5 gados, galvenokārt pateicoties zemākam KASKO un labākai amortizācijai. Domāju, ka jūsu klientiem tas varētu noderēt pirms lēmuma. Vai vari pārskatīt 15 min šonedēļ? honestcost.lv
TipRun the comparison before you send. The number has to be real. If their car loses on TCO, switch the competitor until it wins — or don't send the DM. Never lie about the math.

Touch 03 · The single follow-up
~50 words · LV · reply to your own thread
Two sentences. One closing question. Never more.
Goal: reply or move on. Send 5 days after Touch 02 if no answer. After this — silence. No third email.
[Vārds], viena papildu rinda. Saprotu, ka maijs ir intensīvs. Ja HonestCost neder šim brīdim — pasaki, izņemšu no saraksta. Ja interesants, bet citā mēnesī — pasaki, kad būs labāks. honestcost.lv
TipGiving someone permission to say no is the single most-effective unstuck move. ~40% of "no replies" reply to this exact wording.

The demo itself — 30 minutes max

  • Open the live site, not the slides. Slides are the leave-behind. The demo is the product. Start by running their car vs the strongest competitor.
  • Make them do the typing. Hand them the laptop. They see how easy it is for their customer.
  • Show the trust section. Open "Kā mēs aprēķinām", scroll the sources. This is what addresses "is this credible enough to put on our site?".
  • Show the share link. Send the comparison to their email in front of them. They feel the virality.
  • End on price, not features. "€200, first month free. I set it up by Friday. Yes or no by next Tuesday?" Direct ask. No squirm room.
  • Never discount in the room. If they push back on price: "Let me think about it and come back tomorrow." Then come back at €200 with one extra feature (logo placement, custom CTA).

If they say no

Three possible "no" patterns. Each has a 30-second response:

ObjectionReadReply (LV)
"Mēs jau taisām paši" Bluff. They don't. "Saproti. Vai vari rādīt linku? Es vēlos saprast, kā jūs to risināt — mans rīks varbūt der citur."
"Budžets šobrīd nav" Likely true, but soft. "Saproti. Vai €99/mēnesī pirmajiem 3 mēnešiem ar 6 mēnešu prepay būtu skatāms? Es vēlos jūsu loģo, ne jūsu naudu."
"Vajag saskaņot ar HQ" True for BMW / VW. Pivot to pilot. "Saproti. Vai varam darīt 1 mēneša pilotu bez maksas, lai tev būtu kas rādīt HQ? Es uzturu, jūs lietojat, vērtējat."
"Nezinu, vai mūsu klientiem tas vajadzīgs" Real uncertainty. Show data. "Pēdējie 30 dienas — X lietotāji salīdzinājuši jūsu segmenta auto, Y minūtes vidējais skatīšanās laiks. Ja vēlies, atsūtu pilnu raportu."

The 1-pager case study (build after first close)

Within 30 days of signing your first dealer, build a one-page case study. Format:

  • Dealer logo, full-width, top
  • One sentence summary ("Greenmotors integrated HonestCost on greenmotors.lv on June 1st.")
  • Three stats: leads/month, cost/lead, time-on-result vs site average
  • One pull quote from the marketing manager (give them the wording, they'll tweak)
  • "Want the same? — [Tavs vārds], [tel], [email]"

This single PDF unlocks Tier 1 outreach. Without it, BMW / Möller will not engage. With it, you have a 15-minute meeting on the calendar within a week.
