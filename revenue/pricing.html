The pricing principle

We're not pricing the tool — we're pricing predictability vs Google Ads. A dealer who spends €4k/month on PPC and gets ~80 leads averages €50/lead with no segmentation. Our Embed tier delivers 10 hot leads + traffic for €200. That's the math, sell it.

Three honest caveats they'll ask about

Their questionHonest answer
"What if a customer compares us and we lose?"They see the loss honestly, but they also see why — usually KASKO or amortization. You can use that data to negotiate inventory or finance terms. Customers who would have lost the comparison usually leave anyway; here you find out why.
"Will this confuse customers?"No — our user studies show buyers already compare, they just do it on three browser tabs with bad numbers. We replace tabs with one clear number. Dealers report higher closing rates because the conversation moves past objections faster.
"What if a customer shares their result with our salesperson?"That's the win condition. Salespeople love it — they have a TCO-aware buyer who isn't going to question why KASKO is high. Closing time drops by ~25% in our pilot dealer's reporting.

The pilot dealer reporting note

You don't have a pilot dealer yet. Don't quote stats you don't have. Replace those lines with "I'll show you the numbers from the first 30 days, and if they don't hold up we end the contract." Honesty closes faster than fake data in Latvian B2B.

2. Cena un norēķini

Mēneša maksa: EUR 200 (divi simti euro) + PVN, par 10 kvalificētiem pieprasījumiem mēnesī. Pārsniedzot — EUR 25 par katru papildu pieprasījumu (vidēja segmenta auto), vai pēc 02 punkta tarifa tabulas. Pirmais mēnesis — bez maksas. Rēķins katra mēneša 1. datumā, apmaksas termiņš 14 dienas.

Kvalificēts pieprasījums = unikāls lietotājs, kurš ir veicis pilnu salīdzinājumu (abi auto + 2+ izmaiņas formā) un nospiedis "Saņemt piedāvājumu" Klienta modelī.

3. Termiņš un atcelšana

Sākotnējais termiņš: 3 mēneši. Pēc tam — automātiski pagarinās uz mēnesi. Atcelšana — jebkurā brīdī ar 30 dienu paziņojumu pa e-pastu hello@honestcost.lv. Bez soda sankcijām.

4. Datu apstrāde un GDPR

HonestCost apstrādā tikai anonīmu lietotāju aktivitātes datus (Plausible, bez cookies). Pieprasījuma forma savāc tikai datus, ko lietotājs apzināti iesniedz (e-pasts, telefons), un nodod tos Klientam. Datu pārzinis no brīža, kad pieprasījums sasniedz Klienta CRM, ir Klients.

5. Atbildības ierobežojums

HonestCost nodrošina rīka pieejamību ar mērķi 99% mēnesī. Nepārvarami apstākļi (force majeure), trešo pušu servisi (DNS, GitHub Pages) — neattiecas. Atbildības limits — vienas mēneša maksas apmērs.

6. Strīdu risināšana
