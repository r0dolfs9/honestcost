# HonestCost UGC Reveal Ad Design

## Summary

Create a 25-30 second vertical TikTok/Reels-style UGC reveal ad for HonestCost. The first public-facing angle is a skeptical friend discovering that the car with the lower sticker price can cost more per month once full ownership costs are included.

Working title: "Pag, ka tas ir letak?"

Primary language: Latvian.

Ending mood: UGC discovery for most of the video, then a short cinematic "movie trailer" style product reveal with "DRIZUMA".

## Goals

- Introduce HonestCost as a coming-soon product without overexplaining it.
- Make the hook emotionally simple: the cheap-looking car is not always cheaper.
- Show the main product value: compare two cars and see the real monthly cost.
- Flash supporting features: car picker/prefill database, saved scenarios, shareable comparison, and FAQ/methodology.
- Feel native to TikTok/Reels rather than like a polished corporate ad.

## Audience

Latvian car buyers who are comparing used or new cars and do not fully trust sticker price as the deciding number. The creator persona is a skeptical friend who has just noticed something surprising and wants to show it quickly.

## Core Message

Before buying a car, compare the full monthly reality: fuel, insurance, tax, service, depreciation, and ownership assumptions.

Short message:

"Cena salonā nav reālā mēneša cena."

Fallback ASCII version for edits:

"Cena salona nav reala menesa cena."

## Format

- Platform: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts.
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical.
- Duration: 25-30 seconds.
- Style: handheld selfie plus screen recording, with quick zooms and native captions.
- Pace: fast but legible.
- Music: low, tense trailer-like pulse under the final 6-8 seconds. Earlier section can use quiet native background audio.

## Storyboard

### 0-3s: Selfie Hook

Visual:

Creator holds phone or films themselves beside a laptop/phone screen.

Voice:

"Pag... ka masina ar zemaku cenu beigas sanak dargaka menesi?"

On-screen text:

"Pag. Ka sis ir dargaks menesi?"

### 3-10s: HonestCost Comparison

Visual:

Screen recording of HonestCost with two cars side by side. The user taps the car picker and prefills both vehicles.

Voice:

"Es domaju, ka pirmais variants ir drosi letaks. Bet tad ieliku abus HonestCost."

On-screen text:

"Cena salona nav reala menesa cena"

Feature shown:

Two-car comparison and car picker/prefill.

### 10-17s: Result Reveal

Visual:

Monthly cost result becomes the center of attention. Add a quick zoom or pointer to the total.

Voice:

"Te nav tikai pirkuma cena. Te ir degviela, KASKO, nodoklis, serviss, vertibas kritums... viss kopa."

On-screen text hits:

"Degviela"
"KASKO"
"Nodoklis"
"Serviss"
"Vertibas kritums"

Feature shown:

Full monthly ownership-cost result.

### 17-22s: Feature Flashes

Visual:

Fast cuts through supporting product moments:

- Car picker/prefill database.
- Ownership horizon controls.
- Saved scenario.
- FAQ/methodology page.

Voice:

"Un tu vari saglabat salidzinajumu un parbaudit, no kurienes nak aprekinu pienemumi."

On-screen text hits:

"Salidzini"
"Saglabā"
"Parbaudi pienemumus"

Feature shown:

Saved scenarios/shareability and methodology trust.

### 22-30s: Trailer Reveal Ending

Visual:

Screen darkens or cuts to a clean HonestCost wordmark. The final beat should feel cinematic, but still simple.

Voice:

"Pirms perc auto, uzzini ta patieso cenu."

On-screen text:

"HONESTCOST"
"DRIZUMA"

Optional final micro-copy:

"Patiesa auto cena. Pirms lemuma."

## Production Notes

- Keep the creator's delivery slightly skeptical, not salesy.
- Do not claim legal compliance, dealer partnerships, live custom domain, monitoring, or verified data freshness unless those are proven at production time.
- If current product data is used in screenshots, avoid framing exact numbers as universal truth. The tool should be presented as an indicative comparison.
- Show the actual product UI, not abstract graphics.
- Use native captions throughout; most viewers will watch without sound.
- The ending can borrow trailer rhythm, but should not become a full parody that distracts from the product.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Video runs between 25 and 30 seconds.
- Latvian is the primary language.
- First three seconds include a clear skeptical hook.
- HonestCost product UI appears before second 10.
- The main comparison/result is visible and understandable.
- At least three supporting feature flashes appear before the ending.
- Final frame includes "HONESTCOST" and "DRIZUMA".
- No unproven claims are made about launch status, partnerships, legal compliance, or data verification.
