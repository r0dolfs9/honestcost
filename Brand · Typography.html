Type System · 01

Type that
reads like a price tag.

Three families. One job each. Big condensed numerals where money lives, friendly Inter Tight for everything else, monospace where data needs to line up. Quiet, technical, and slightly automotive — like a configurator built by people who care.

05 · Rules

Do this. Not that.

A handful of guard-rails that keep the type system from drifting into "another fintech dashboard".

Do

Pair Condensed display with Tight body.

Barlow Condensed is loud and automotive — let Inter Tight do the calm explaining underneath.

€8 640 ietaupījums
salīdzinot ar BMW X1, 5 gadi, 15 000 km/g
Don't

Never set body copy in the display face.

Barlow Condensed at body sizes is cramped and shouty. Save it for the things you'd say out loud.

Salīdzini divus jaunus automobiļus, ieskaitot amortizāciju, KASKO un transporta nodokli — viss vienā mēneša skaitlī.
Do

Use mono for anything that compares.

Tabular numerals in DM Mono. Columns line up, scanning is instant, the eye trusts it more.

BMW 118i€ 487 /mēn
BMW X1 xDrive20i€ 631 /mēn

Don't

Never put numbers in proportional body.

Inter Tight's '1' is narrower than its '8'. Columns will wobble. Eyes will distrust.

BMW 118i€487 /mēn
BMW X1 xDrive20i€631 /mēn
Do

Use the mono kicker for technical context.

Section numbers, units, source tags — anything that says "this is reference, not voice".

04 · numerals · wltp 2026
Don't

Don't tint headlines. Ever.

Black ink, full stop. Color belongs on numerical winners, badges, and accents — not on display type.
